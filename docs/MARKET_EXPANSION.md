## ğŸ“ Lessons from Comparable Companies

### Duolingo (Language Learning)
- **Success:** Freemium model + localization + gamification
- **Lesson:** Start with one market, perfect product-market fit, then expand
- **Application:** Perfect US market before LATAM expansion

### Coursera (Online Education)
- **Success:** Partner with local universities for credibility
- **Lesson:** Local partnerships accelerate trust and adoption
- **Application:** Partner with top universities in each market

### Nubank (Brazilian Fintech)
- **Success:** Solved local payment pain points (Pix, installments)
- **Lesson:** Understanding local payment culture is critical
- **Application:** Implement Pix and boleto from day one in Brazil

### Mercado Libre (LATAM E-commerce)
- **Success:** Country-by-country rollout with local operations
- **Lesson:** Each LATAM market requires tailored approach
- **Application:** Don't treat LATAM as monolithicâ€”localize deeply
