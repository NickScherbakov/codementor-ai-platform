# Code of Conduct

## Our standards

We are committed to a welcoming, safe, and constructive community.

Expected behavior:
- Be respectful and professional.
- Give actionable, good-faith feedback.
- Assume positive intent and ask clarifying questions.
- Focus criticism on ideas and code, not people.

Unacceptable behavior:
- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Trolling, doxxing, threats, or intimidation.
- Repeated disruptive behavior after moderator requests.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close threads, or block participation for violations.

## Reporting

Report incidents via the security/support channels listed in [SUPPORT.md](SUPPORT.md).
